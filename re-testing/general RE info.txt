MESSAGE DATA LAYOUT:
0-11: nonce
12-(^16-1): encrypted data
^16-(EOF-1): tag
^ means from end of message

the nonce is randomly generated before every encryption

aes mode of operation is GCM

aes-256 secret:
0x4C, 0x48, 0x77, 0x55, 0x47, 0x6E, 0x6B, 0x74,
0x43, 0x6C, 0x4E, 0x76, 0x39, 0x55, 0x6F, 0x63,
0x31, 0x47, 0x71, 0x7A, 0x63, 0x63, 0x62, 0x68,
0x72, 0x64, 0x61, 0x4A, 0x33, 0x41, 0x06, 0x0A

vectors sent from the game to server are sent as integers with a fixed accuracy. to get the real value divide the value by 100_000.
